About

19 years making images that speak.

I’m Daniel Cal, a 3D generalist based in Valencia, Spain. Since 2007 I’ve been turning ideas, blueprints and sketches into images that communicate, convince and inspire.

My speciality is architectural visualisation and vehicle simulation, though I’ve worked on interior design, product and animation projects for clients across Spain.

The last 10 years I’ve worked as a freelancer, giving me a complete project vision: from briefing to delivery. I’m now looking to join a stable team where I can keep growing.

Process

How I work

01
Briefing
Plans, references, intended use and deadlines. I ask early about the format (still, animation, real-time) to shape every decision that follows.
02
Modelling
Built in Blender, with ZBrush for organic detail. Polygon budget matched to the target engine — simulator assets stay lean; archviz can go deeper.
03
Render
Blender Cycles for photorealistic stills. EEVEE or Unreal Engine when real-time output is needed. Materials authored in Substance Painter or Blender nodes.
04
Delivery
Post-production in Affinity Photo. Client review round included. Final files in the agreed formats and resolutions, with source files on request.
